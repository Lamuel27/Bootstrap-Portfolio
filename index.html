I currently work at a Fintech company call ClickSWITCH. ClickSWITCH is an automated account switching solution that revolutionizes the way financial institutions attract and retain new primary account holders. This unique customer acquisition technology automates, streamlines and simplifies the process of bringing new account holders onboard. ClickSWITCH quickly, safely and efficiently switches account holders recurring payments from their old accounts to new ones. I believe I have a lot to contribute when it comes to building our application, so once I finish this course I hope to move to our development team.
